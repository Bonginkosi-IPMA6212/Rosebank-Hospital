This document outlines the standard operating procedures for IT managers at Rosebank Hospital

2. Responsibilities

  • Oversee IT operations and infrastructure.
  • Manage IT staff and resources effectively.
  • Ensure compliance with healthcare regulations.
  • Maintain data security and integrity.

3. Incident Management

  • Log all IT incidents in the ticketing system.
  • Prioritize incidents based on severity and impact.
  • Assign incidents to appropriate staff for resolution.
  • Follow up on incidents until resolved and document outcomes.

4. Change Management

  • Submit change requests for all significant IT modifications.
  • Conduct risk assessments before implementing changes.
  • Notify stakeholders of upcoming changes.
  • Review and document the results of changes.

5. Data Backup and Recovery

  • Implement regular data backup schedules.
  • Test backup procedures quarterly.
  • Ensure offsite storage of critical backups.
  • Establish a disaster recovery plan.

6. Staff Training

  • Conduct regular training sessions on IT policies and procedures.
  • Provide onboarding for new IT staff.
  • Encourage continuous learning and professional development.
