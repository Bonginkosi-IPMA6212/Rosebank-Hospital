Acquisition Procedures

Identify Needs

  • Determine and record the precise medical supplies, equipment, or services that are needed.

  • During the requirement-defining process, include the appropriate departments (such as the procurement team and healthcare personnel).

Conduct Market Research

  • Research potential suppliers and vendors.

  • assemble data regarding products that are offered, costs, and industry norms.

  • Examine the track records and reputations of suppliers.

Develop Procurement Plan

  • Specify your procurement approach (direct purchase, competitive bidding, etc).

  • Set budget limits and timeline for acquisition.

  • Establish evaluation criteria (e.g., cost, quality, compliance with health regulations).

Prepare and Issue Solicitation

  • Draft and issue Request for Proposals (RFPs), Invitations to Bid (ITBs), or Requests for Quotations (RFQs) to potential vendors.

  • Include detailed specifications, terms, and conditions.

Receive and Review Proposals/Bids

  • Collect and organize submitted proposals or bids.

  • Form an evaluation committee to review and score based on predefined criteria.

  • Check for compliance with hospital policies and regulatory requirements.

Select and Award Contract

  • Choose the supplier or vendor offering the best value based on evaluation.
  • Negotiate contract terms and conditions.
  • Finalize and sign the contract.

Implement and Manage Contract

  • Monitor supplier performance to ensure compliance with contract terms.
  • Coordinate delivery, installation, and training as needed.
  • Address any issues or discrepancies promptly.

Ensure Compliance and Documentation

  • Maintain accurate records of procurement activities and contracts.
  • Ensure all procedures comply with hospital policies, healthcare regulations, and legal requirements.

Review and Evaluate

  • Assess the acquisition process and outcomes.
  • Collect feedback from stakeholders and identify areas for improvement.
  • Implement lessons learned in future procurement activities.
